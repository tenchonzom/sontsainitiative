VOLUNTEER WITH SONTSA

Are you interested in helping the Tibetan youth? Volunteer with us!

MENTOR

We're looking for Tibetan university students in the US that have gone through the process of applying to college in the US. We prefer mentors that went to high school in India as most of our mentees come from this background, but anybody that can help with the US college application process should apply!

MENTEE

We hope to welcome an amazing group of bright Tibetan youths in exile committed to the goals of pursuing four year Undergraduate eduction in the United States. Through the program, you will get to know amazing friends, mentors, and a community of dedicated Tibetan youths. We are looking forward to getting know you all!
